
Fraud Rate (%)
Definition: (Total Fraudulent Transactions / Total Transactions) * 100

Business Purpose: This is the primary health indicator. We monitor this against a 1.5% VAMP (Value At Risk) threshold to identify spikes in malicious activity before they impact merchant standing.

Average Velocity
Definition: AVG(Timestamp of Purchase - Timestamp of Signup)

Business Purpose: Behavioral heuristic used to identify bot attacks. A sudden decrease in average velocity often indicates scripted account creation and immediate "carding" attempts.

Alert Accuracy (%)
Definition: (Count of High Priority Alerts confirmed as Fraud / Total High Priority Alerts)

Business Purpose: Measures the precision of our detection logic. High accuracy ensures that analysts are spending their time on actual threats rather than clearing false positives.

Chargeback Win Rate (%)
Definition: (Count of Won Disputes / Total Chargeback Cases)

Business Purpose: Measures the effectiveness of our evidence collection and dispute process in recovering lost revenue from fraudulent claims.
